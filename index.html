Our Focus

QCSA is an undergraduate/graduate organization for students interested in quantum computing to enter the field and learn the fundamentals of quantum computing, discuss quantum computing research with CQSE faculty members and work on projects and pick up useful tools for a career in quantum computing

qBraid Quantum Computing High School Summer course

The UCLA undergraduate Quantum Computing Club is partnering with Quantum Computing (QC) startup qBraid to teach high school students the basics of QC. The class will run for 10 weeks during the summer, meeting once a week over zoom, outside of school hours. No computing or physics background is required. The only prerequisites are high school Algebra, access to the internet, and curiosity about quantum computing.
